# 📊 Global Sales Performance & Profitability Analysis (Excel Dashboard)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes a large-scale global sales dataset (10,000+ records) to uncover key business insights related to revenue, profitability, customer behavior, and operational efficiency using Excel.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Identify revenue and profit drivers  
- Analyze customer segments and purchasing behavior  
- Evaluate delivery, returns, and operational performance  
- Build an interactive dashboard for decision-making  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Excel (Advanced)  
- Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts  
- Slicers & Dashboards  
- Conditional Formatting  
- Formulas: XLOOKUP, SUMIFS, INDEX-MATCH  

---

## 📂 Dataset Description
- 10,000+ records  
- 30 variables including:
  - Sales, Profit  
  - Customer Demographics  
  - Delivery Performance  
  - Discounts & Returns  
  - Multi-region & multi-channel data  

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis
- Revenue analysis by region, country, and category  
- Profitability analysis (profit & margin)  
- Customer segmentation analysis  
- Delivery & logistics performance  
- Return rate and risk analysis  
- Discount impact on profit

## 💡 Key Business Insights

### 📊 Revenue
- Certain regions drive the majority of revenue  
- Few categories dominate total sales  
- Seasonal demand patterns observed  

### 📈 Profit
- High revenue ≠ high profit due to discounts  
- Some categories have strong margins  
- Heavy discounts reduce profitability  

### 👥 Customer
- Specific segments contribute most revenue  
- Age-based purchasing trends identified  

### 🚚 Operations
- Delivery delays impact customer satisfaction  
- Warehouse performance varies significantly  

### 🔁 Returns
- High return rates reduce revenue and profit  
- Product issues linked to higher returns  

### 🏷️ Discounts
- Discounts increase sales but reduce margins  
- Moderate discounts perform better  

---

## ⚠️ Key Problems Identified
- High discount dependency  
- Delivery delays  
- High return rates  
- Uneven regional performance  

---

## 🚀 Final Outcome
Developed an interactive Excel dashboard delivering actionable insights and enabling data-driven decision-making.
